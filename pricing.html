Common Questions

Is there a minimum hour requirement? expand_more
Yes, we generally require a minimum visit of 3 hours to ensure high-quality care continuity for our clients and stability for our caregivers.
Do you direct bill insurance? expand_more
We can direct bill Alberta Blue Cross and select other providers. For others, we provide detailed receipts for quick reimbursement.
Can I change my plan later? expand_more
Absolutely. Care needs change, and we are flexible. You can adjust the level of care or hours with 48 hours notice.
Are caregivers insured and bonded? expand_more
Yes, every KapwaCare professional is fully vetted, background-checked, insured, and bonded for your peace of mind.
Do you offer overnight or weekend care? expand_more
Yes! We offer care 7 days a week, including evenings, overnights, and holidays. Rates may vary for overnight and holiday shifts.
